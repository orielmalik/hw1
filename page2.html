The Eiffel Tower, a wrought-iron lattice tower in Paris, France, reigned as the world's tallest structure for 40 years (1889-1930). Designed by Gustave Eiffel, it's a Parisian landmark and popular tourist destination.

The Louvre Museum is one of the largest and most famous museums in the world. It houses a vast collection of artworks, including the Mona Lisa, Venus de Milo, and Winged Victory of Samothrace. The museum is located just a short walk from the Eiffel Tower, across the River Seine.
