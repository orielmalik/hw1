Persons who connected to beckingham palace

Prince William: Heir apparent to the British throne, Prince William is the elder son of King Charles III and the late Princess Diana. He is known for his charitable work and focus on environmental issues.

Queen Elizabeth II: Britain's longest-reigning monarch, Queen Elizabeth II passed away in September 2022. During her historic 70-year reign, she witnessed immense social and political change.

Prince Harry: Duke of Sussex, Prince Harry is the younger son of King Charles III and Princess Diana. He stepped back from his senior royal duties in 2020 and now resides in the United States with his wife Meghan Markle.
